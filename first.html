Some ideas around what should be recognized

We are familiar with well-known EPAM values and pillars as a part of our EPAM DNA and daily routine.

But sometimes it's better to understand very easy behaviors what does it all mean. Exactly for that reason we would like to provide you with some ideas what could be taken into consideration during creating/ adjusting your customized recognition programs.
