GDO VS Recognition Landscape

At EPAM we really believe that appreciation makes the difference. That is why we do our best to ensure sufficient recognition approaches to thank people for efforts and contributions. The whole set of Recognition activities at EPAM forms the Global Recognition Landscape consisting of several layers each represented by a number of dedicated Recognition Programs.

Global programs

are those operating globally according to universal rules and available for all EPAMers. EPAM Impact, Service Anniversary are among them. The details about Global Recognition Programs you can find here.

Location programs

include programs designed for certain groups united by location or region. In our case we speak about countries, included in GDO VS.

Custom programs

cover all initiatives designed for certain groups united by unit, account, project, office or knowledge domain/area/role, which are implemented withing GDO VS.
